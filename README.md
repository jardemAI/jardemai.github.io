# Järdem AI

## Simple, open-source AI help for families in Central Asia

**Järdem AI is an open-source, privacy-first helper. It protects families from scams, translates complex documents into plain language, and helps people navigate public services — in Kazakh, Russian, and mixed languages.**

Designed for families, parents, elderly relatives, and the local builders (NGOs, civic-tech, developers) who support them.

## Why we are building Järdem AI

Most AI tools are made for English-speaking, tech-savvy users with stable internet. 

But in Central Asia, everyday life is different. People often deal with:
* **Clever scams:** Fake bank links, SMS, and suspicious WhatsApp calls. One wrong click can cost a family their savings.
* **Confusing bureaucracy:** Government instructions and legal documents written in heavy, formal language.
* **Mixed languages:** People easily mix Kazakh, Russian, slang, and voice messages. Standard AI expects perfect English prompts.
* **Privacy worries:** Sensitive life situations (finances, documents, health) shouldn't be sent to closed corporate servers.

Järdem AI is designed to solve these exact problems.

## What Järdem AI does

### 1. Explains documents in normal human language
Paste any official text, certificate, or instruction. Järdem AI breaks it down into a simple, clear checklist.
> *Example:* “Explain this divorce certificate process in simple Russian.” / “Қарапайым тілмен түсіндірші: бұл құжат не туралы?”

### 2. Spots scams and fake links
Paste a suspicious SMS, WhatsApp message, or website link. Järdem AI checks it for common fraud patterns and warns you if it’s dangerous.
> *Example:* “My bank sent this link, is it real?” / “Маған мынандай сілтеме келді. Тексеріп берші.”

### 3. Guides you through public services
Järdem AI gives simple, step-by-step guides for everyday tasks: opening an individual business, changing documents, or applying for social benefits. It doesn't replace a lawyer, but it tells you exactly where to start and what to ask.

### 4. Understands how people actually speak
You don't need to write perfect, formal prompts. Järdem AI understands Kazakh, Russian, mixed speech, typos, and short, messy questions.

### 5. Works offline and respects your privacy
Our long-term goal is a **local-first** assistant. It should run on lightweight open models that don't depend on closed APIs and can work even with unstable internet.

## Roadmap

* **Phase 1 (Current):** Public web demo with 3 basic scenarios (scams, documents, public services) for Kazakhstan.
* **Phase 2:** Open-source prototype, initial safety filters, and a small local dataset.
* **Phase 3:** Lightweight model experiments (local-first AI) and mixed-language testing.
* **Phase 4:** Support for Uzbek and Kyrgyz languages, developer tools, and NGO partnerships.

## Who this helps

* **Families & Elderly relatives:** Those who are most vulnerable to online fraud and find online forms scary.
* **Regular citizens:** Anyone trying to understand a confusing official letter or fine.
* **Local builders:** NGOs, civic-tech teams, and developers who need free, open-source AI tools built for Central Asian languages.
